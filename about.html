About this notebook

Half-Baked Thoughts is a running collection of ideas — health and nutrition, food, geopolitics, family memories, gardening, and whatever else is rattling around that doesn't fit neatly anywhere else. Nothing here claims to be finished; most of it is closer to thinking out loud than a polished take.

There's no single theme on purpose. Some days it's a note on soil and compost, other days it's a half-formed idea about shipping lanes, other days it's just a memory that surfaced out of nowhere. The dashboard on the homepage is built so all of it is visible at a glance, sorted by topic, without needing five different sites to keep track of.

New entries get added over time — thanks for reading along.
